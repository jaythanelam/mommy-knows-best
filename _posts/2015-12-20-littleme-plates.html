Everyone knows exactly which set is theirs so knows what and how much of everything they're getting and how to get that to the table and set!  It's brilliant!

These Little Me plates are wonderful! They are microwaveable, dishwasher safe and have lasted for several key cycles through our family meals. Each of my kiddos has a plate and bowl set! Check it out here!

But most importantly, LittleMe's customer service cannot be beat!
