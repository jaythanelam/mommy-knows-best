Once we caught on to this trend, we pressed our advantage and introduced every random finger food and veggie we could find in front of our kiddos, classing it up with a small dollop of ranch. It was on this path that I found these brilliant little plate/dipper combos from BOON! The small bowls fit on the lip of the plate and make perfect portions for your little dipper!
